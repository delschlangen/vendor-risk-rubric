    Risk Dimensions

    1. Co-Employment Risk

    0

    Risk that vendor workers could be legally classified as employees

    0123
    0: No vendor personnel on-site; purely transactional (SaaS, product)
    1: Occasional on-site; vendor-supervised; clear deliverables
    2: Regular on-site; some team integration; shared tools
    3: Embedded daily; company directs work; uses company email/badge

    2. Data Sensitivity & Privacy

    0

    Risk related to vendor access to sensitive, regulated, or proprietary data

    0123
    0: No access to company data
    1: Internal business data (non-sensitive); no PII
    2: PII, customer data, or contractually confidential data
    3: Regulated data (HIPAA, GDPR, financial), trade secrets, source code

    3. Physical Safety & Injury Exposure

    0

    Risk of physical harm arising from vendor activities

    0123
    0: Remote/virtual only; no physical presence
    1: On-site in office environment; standard hazards
    2: Equipment, vehicles, or moderate physical risk
    3: High-risk: construction, electrical, heights, confined spaces

    4. Site & Security Access

    0

    Risk from vendor access to physical facilities or IT systems

    0123
    0: No facility or system access
    1: Escorted facility access; read-only or no IT access
    2: Unescorted access; badge issued; non-production IT
    3: Data center, secure areas, or production admin access

    5. Business Continuity Impact

    0

    Risk that vendor failure would disrupt critical operations

    0123
    0: Easily replaceable; commoditized; no dependency
    1: Minor inconvenience; 2+ alternatives available
    2: Moderate switching cost; 30-90 day transition
    3: Single point of failure; >90 day transition; critical to revenue

    6. AI/ML Model Risk

    0

    Risk from vendor use of AI/ML in delivered services

    0123
    0: No AI/ML components
    1: Embedded AI; no customization; no training on your data
    2: Custom/fine-tuned models; influences decisions
    3: Trained on your data; affects customers/employees; IP concerns

# Vendor Risk Assessment: Low-Risk SaaS

**Vendor Name:** Diagramify
**Service:** Cloud-based diagramming and flowchart tool
**Requestor:** Marketing
**Department:** Marketing Operations
**Assessment Date:** 2024-12-15
**Assessor:** Risk & Compliance

---

## Vendor Overview

Diagramify is a cloud-based diagramming tool (similar to Lucidchart/Miro) that the marketing team wants to use for creating flowcharts, org charts, and presentation diagrams. The tool would be accessed via browser; no integration with internal systems required. Team would create diagrams containing internal business processes and marketing strategies.

## Key Takeaways

This example demonstrates a **typical low-risk SaaS tool** that should flow through quickly. The framework correctly identifies this as Standard tier, avoiding unnecessary bureaucracy for commodity tools. Key factors keeping it low-risk:

- No system integrations
- No sensitive data (diagrams of processes, not actual data)
- Easily replaceable
- No vendor personnel

**Note:** If the team later requested SSO integration or started storing customer data in diagrams, the assessment should be updated.
